Construction Data For Better Experience

Overview
BCI Central's LeadManager application has been a game-changer for the construction industry, helping companies stay up-to-date on the latest construction project information and manage their customer relationships more effectively. The application's real-time data and CRM capabilities have helped construction companies improve their sales processes and make more informed decisions about which projects to pursue.

We as a team have been working on improving the application experience and adding features since this application was reconstructed in 2021. From the design team we submitted visual improvements, such as fundamentals of color, typography to make it more prominent, and also some guidelines for users to use it better again.

LeadManager is used more frequently by employees working in the construction sector with an average age of +30, as much as possible we give the impression of a corporate app with an attractive mix of colors so that users can be more familiar with using the application. And this application is mostly just text elements, we make it interesting as much as possible to provide construction data.
