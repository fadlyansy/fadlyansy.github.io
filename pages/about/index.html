About Me
Hi! I'm Fadly, currently working as a Senior Product Designer at HappyFresh. I have gained experience working with various startups and corporate companies in fields such as HR Systems, Health Tech, eCommerce, eGroceries, Construction Leads Systems, and also freelance projects with small companies/agency. I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology and specialized in Multimedia Design during my time in Vocational High School.

My journey in Product Design (UI/UX) began in 2017 when I transitioned from a Graphic Design background. I consistently follow a comprehensive design process, starting with problem statement identification, crystalizing the problem into innovative ideas, and developing the design solutions.

I find great joy in my work and hope to have the opportunity to collaborate with you in the future!
